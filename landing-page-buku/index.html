FAQ

How fast do signals travel down a neuron?

Signals can travel down neurons at speeds ranging from 0.5 meters/second up to 120 meters/second, depending on the type of neuron and whether it's myelinated.

Can we really enhance our memories during sleep?

Yes, research shows that proper sleep is essential for memory consolidation. The book details specific techniques to optimize this natural process.

How long does it take the brain to decide?

Simple decisions can take as little as 200 milliseconds, while complex decisions may take several seconds or longer. The book explores factors that influence decision speed and quality.

Using big data to talk to the brain

Modern neuroscience increasingly uses machine learning and big data to decode brain activity patterns. The book explains how these technologies are advancing our understanding of the brain.
